Pets change our lives.

We're on a quest to change theirs.

Quest for Paws Animal Rescue and Sanctuary is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing, fostering, and rehoming the abandoned, sick, and injured animals around our city.

With you help, we hope to make a difference in the lives of these wonderful animals.

How We Help

Animal Rescue

We embark on missions to rescue and recover stray dogs and cats roaming our streets. Every effort is made to ensure these animals find safety and care.

Veterinary Services

We provide free veterinary treatment to strays, rescues, and pets. Our services include spaying and neutering to help control the pet population and prevent future strays. We also offer dental care, parasite control, and nutritional advice to keep pets healthy and happy. For animals with chronic conditions or injuries, we provide ongoing care and rehabilitation.

Fostering and Adoption

We help our rescues find their temporary and forever homes. We work diligently to match pets with their perfect forever families. Our adoption process is thorough, ensuring that each pet goes to a home that suits their personality and needs. We provide adopters with all the necessary resources and support to ensure a smooth transition for both the pet and their new family. Through our efforts, we aim to reduce the number of homeless animals and promote responsible pet ownership.

How You Can Help

There are countless ways you can help us in our quest for paws. You can make a donation (however big or small), join our fundraiser events, or volunteer your time and home as a foster parent.

Welcome to Our Adoption Process

Thank you for considering adoption. Here are the steps to help you bring a new member into your family.

Steps to Adopt

  1. Step 1: Initial Inquiry

    Contact us to express your interest in adoption. We will provide you with an application form.

  2. Step 2: Submit Application

    Fill out and submit the application form with all the required documents.

  3. Step 3: Home Study

    Our team will schedule a home visit to discuss your interest and evaluate your home environment.

  4. Step 4: Approval

    Once approved, you will be matched with a child based on your preferences and home study report.

  5. Step 5: Placement

    The child will be placed in your home for a trial period before finalizing the adoption.

  6. Step 6: Finalization

    After the trial period, legal paperwork will be completed to finalize the adoption.

Requirements for Adoption

  • Completed application form
  • Background check
  • Home study report
  • References
  • Proof of income

Necessary Documents

  • Proof of identity (passport, driver's license)
  • Proof of income (tax returns, pay stubs)
  • Medical records
  • Personal references
